Is pitching helpful?

No
Definitely no

The pitch process has been engrained in the creative industry for years, so I’m sure you’re familiar with the story.

A brief comes in.

You have a ridiculously small amount of time to prepare.

You’re put in the spotlight

57% of the time you’re cast aside.

It’s NOT feasible. Or desirable.

Why free pitching doesn’t work

It devalues you and your expertise

Providing creative work with a minimal brief, zero client collaboration and little research gives a skewed impression of what it takes to create great work.

You risk being ripped off

Don’t be naive - there’s every possibility you’ll be used to spark some creative flare amongst internal teams who will take your idea and execute it cheaply.

It costs money and resources

Pitch work takes time and energy away from real clients. Not to mention, your team is investing into something that will likely be discarded.
